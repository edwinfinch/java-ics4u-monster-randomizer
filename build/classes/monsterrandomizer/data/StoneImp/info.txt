Name: Stone Imp
Difficulty: Easy
Connection: None
Summon: StoneImp
Melee: Yes
Distance: None
Type: Demon, Elemental
Brightmaps: No
Added States: No
ACS: No


An Imp variant with no projectile attack. Bit more health than the Imp, but just
as slow and can only melee. It is completely immune to electric/lightning and
poison based-attacks, has decent resistant to fire-based attacks. but slightly weak
against ice-based attacks.
